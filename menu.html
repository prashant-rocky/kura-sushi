Our Signature Menu

Discover the true taste of Japan with our carefully crafted menu at Kura Sushi Dotonbori. From fresh and delicate nigiri to creative sushi rolls, comforting side dishes, and sweet Japanese desserts, every plate is made with passion and authenticity.

Rolls & Maki

Rolls & Maki – A perfect blend of flavors and textures, combining fresh seafood, crisp vegetables, and seasoned rice, rolled with care for a delightful bite every time.

Nigiri Sushi

Nigiri Sushi – Fresh seafood paired with hand-pressed rice, delivering authentic Japanese flavor in every bite.

Dishes & Appetizers

Side Dishes & Appetizers – Light and flavorful starters like edamame and miso soup, perfect companions to your sushi experience.

Desserts & Drinks

Desserts & Drinks – Sweet, refreshing, and satisfying treats to complete your sushi experience, from matcha delights to light Japanese classics.

Kura Sushi Global Flagship Store in Dotonbori, Osaka, offers a unique dining experience with fresh, high-quality sushi served in a fun conveyor belt style.
