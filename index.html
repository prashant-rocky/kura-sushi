A Sushi Journey in the
Heart of Dotonbori

Where Tradition Meets Innovation

Welcome to the Kura Sushi Global Flagship Store in Dotonbori, Osaka. Nestled in the lively heart of the city, our flagship brings together centuries-old sushi traditions and modern dining innovation through our signature conveyor belt system.

From classic nigiri and fresh sashimi to seasonal specialties, every plate is prepared with care and passion. More than a meal, it’s an immersive dining experience designed for families, travelers, and sushi lovers from around the world.
